About Carbon Atlantic

A Bridge Between
Africa and Europe

Carbon Atlantic is a specialist hardwood charcoal trading company connecting premium African producers with European distributors, retailers, and industrial buyers.

We operate at the intersection of supply chain integrity and commercial efficiency. Every shipment is subject to rigorous third-party pre-loading inspection — verifying moisture content, ash levels, fixed carbon percentage, and sizing — before a single container is sealed.

Our model is built on transparency: you know exactly what you're receiving before it leaves the port, with documentation to match every specification agreed upon at order.

Our Services

What We Deliver
Beyond the Product

Quality Control
Pre-Shipment Inspection

Every shipment is verified by an independent third-party inspector at the port of loading. We provide Quality Inspection and Loading Inspection with proven photos of ash, fixed carbon, and sizing reports before the container is sealed — giving you full confidence before payment.

Sourcing
Reliable Origin Supply

We work with established Nigerian producers with proven export operations, logistics infrastructure, and documentation capability — including phytosanitary certificates, certificate of origin, and fumigation.

Documentation
Complete Export Documentation

We handle the full document chain: Bill of Lading, commercial invoice, packing list, weight certificate, inspection certificate, and all compliance documents required for EU customs clearance.

Partnerships
Long-Term Supply Agreements

For buyers requiring consistent monthly or quarterly volumes, we structure supply agreements with fixed pricing windows, priority loading, and guaranteed specification compliance across multiple shipments.

How It Works

From Inquiry to
Delivery

01
Inquiry & Specification

You tell us your required product, volume, Incoterm preference, and target port. We confirm availability and send a formal offer within 48 hours.

02
Order Confirmation

You confirm the proforma invoice and pay the agreed deposit. We issue the purchase order to our supplier and schedule loading dates.

03
Inspection & Loading

Our independent inspector verifies quality and quantity at loading. You receive the full inspection report and photos before the vessel departs.

04
Shipping & Delivery

We send you the complete document package. Your cargo arrives at the agreed European port ready for customs clearance and onward distribution.
